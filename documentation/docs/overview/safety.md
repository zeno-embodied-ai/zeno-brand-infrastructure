# Safety

:::danger Draft / TBD
This page is not a substitute for product-specific safety documentation, local laboratory rules, competition rules or qualified supervision.
:::

Embodied systems can involve electrical energy, moving mechanisms, batteries, tools, water environments and autonomous behavior. Future Zeno procedures must provide product- and version-specific safety requirements before an activity is performed.

## Minimum documentation expectations

- Identify the applicable platform, firmware and control version.
- Define a safe startup, shutdown and recovery path.
- State the required supervision, work area and emergency stop assumptions.
- Validate communication and sensing before motion or mission execution.
- Stop when behavior, wiring, power state or environment differs from the approved procedure.

Specific limits, protective equipment and operating instructions remain TBD.
